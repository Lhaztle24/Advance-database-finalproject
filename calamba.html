Welcome to Calamba, Laguna!

Calamba is a beautiful city located in the province of Laguna, Philippines.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Scenic views and natural attractions
  • Historical landmarks, including the birthplace of Dr. Jose Rizal
  • Rich cultural heritage
  • Accessible location

Cons:

  • Traffic congestion during peak hours
  • Some areas prone to flooding during rainy season
  • Limited public transportation options

Things to Do

  • Visit Rizal Shrine
  • Explore Pansol Hot Springs
  • Enjoy a boat ride at Lake Caliraya
  • Indulge in local delicacies at the public market
