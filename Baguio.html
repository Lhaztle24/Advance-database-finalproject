Exploring Baguio City

Pros and Cons of Baguio City

Pros:

  • Scenic beauty with cool climate
  • Cultural heritage and historical sites
  • Abundance of natural attractions like parks and gardens
  • Rich culinary scene with local and international cuisines
  • Friendly and welcoming local community

Cons:

  • Traffic congestion during peak tourist seasons
  • Higher cost of living compared to some other cities
  • Environmental challenges due to increasing tourism
  • Limited availability of certain amenities in remote areas

Things to Do in Baguio City

  1. Visit Burnham Park for boating and picnics
  2. Explore Mines View Park for scenic views of mines and mountains
  3. Experience the Baguio Night Market for local crafts and food
  4. Learn about Philippine history at the Baguio Museum
  5. Shop for souvenirs at Session Road and SM City Baguio
  6. Enjoy strawberry picking at nearby farms
  7. Try out local delicacies like strawberry taho and ube jam
  8. Hike or trek at nearby trails like Mount Pulag or the Yellow Trail
